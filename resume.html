About Myself:

Hello! My name is Yash Pandey, and I am a passionate web developer with experience in creating dynamic and responsive websites. With a keen eye for detail and a drive for innovation, I thrive on building efficient and user-friendly applications that enhance digital experiences.


I pursued my early education at Gillco International School and am currently working towards my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science & Engineering (BE/CSE) from Chandigarh University, set to graduate in 2027. My academic journey has fueled my enthusiasm for technology, software development, and problem-solving.


Throughout my career, I have worked on several projects, including:


Hotel Management System – A robust platform designed to streamline hotel operations, bookings, and customer management.


E-Commerce Website – A full-fledged online marketplace offering seamless shopping experiences, secure transactions, and efficient user interactions.


Beyond project development, I have also contributed to academic research. I proudly published a review paper titled "Forecasting the Future: A Comprehensive Review of Weather Prediction App", which was presented at the MANIT Bhopal Conference. This work explores the advancements in weather prediction technologies and their impact on forecasting accuracy.


Driven by curiosity and an eagerness to grow, I constantly seek new opportunities to expand my knowledge and refine my skills. My goal is to continue innovating in the field of web development and contribute to the ever-evolving tech industry.


Let's connect and collaborate! I am always open to new ideas, projects, and opportunities. Feel free to reach out to me through my contact information or connect with me on LinkedIn. I look forward to hearing from you!
